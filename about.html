About Us

Discover Our Story

Welcome to ThynkTech - Tech and Device Review Blog, your number one source for all things tech and device reviews. We're dedicated to providing you with the very best insights on the latest gadgets, devices, and technology trends, with an emphasis on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Founded in 2010 by Mike Jerald B. Marzan, ThynkTech has come a long way from its beginnings in San Jose City, Nueva Ecija, Philippines. When Mike first started out, his passion for technology and innovation drove him to create this blog. His goal was simple: to offer high-quality reviews and insights that are both reliable and informative.

Our Mission and Values
